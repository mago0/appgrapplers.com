About

We founded the Appalachian Grapplers Association in early 2024 out of our passion for Jiu Jitsu and a desire to share it with our community. We envisioned a welcoming place where people come together to train, learn from each other, and grow — all at a low cost and with no contracts.

Our dedicated instructors volunteer their time to teach classes and oversee open mat sessions, ensuring that all proceeds go back into the club. This allows us to maintain a high-quality training environment without financial barriers.

Whether you're looking to learn self-defense, improve your fitness, or become part of a supportive and tight-knit community, we're here for it. Join us, and let's push each other to new heights.

See you on the mats!

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

BJJ, or "involuntary yoga", focuses on taking ones opponent down to the ground, gaining a dominant position, and using a number of techniques to force them into submission via joint locks or chokeholds.

Our instructors are experienced and will help you advance your skills whether you're a seasoned practictioner or you've never stepped on the mat.
